How to Play

Movement

Move around with W A S D.

Attacking

Left click in the direction you would like to attack.
You do not need to click on the crabs directly.

Items

You can equip up to 3 items and hold up to 6 items.

If you pick up an item when you have a full inventory, the item will disappear!

You can destroy an item by pressing the red X.

Equip an item by clicking on the item.
Items that you have equipped will be saved upon death. Retrieve them by using the same name.

Prestige

Kill as many crabs as you can before you die! Each crab will give you 1 token. After you die, you can spend these tokens to permanently power-up your character!
